LAST ESCAPE

An Alfred Hitchock inspired game where you either build the coffin... or end in it.

Story

You are the medic/gravedigger of a female prison, where a new inmate has proposed you to help her escape in exchange for money for an operation that
would cure your chronic cataracts.
Carry the necessary materials to your workshop to make a coffin in which she'll escape, but beware of the guards!
They'll start to suspect on you if they see you carrying anything strange, so throw it to the floor before they notice! Staying in your workshop might also help.

You must hurry though, who knows how many days you have left...

Controls

Move your mouse + Right Click to move
Left Click to pick up an item
Middle click to leave it on the ground
Tab to open/close items list
